# Music Tracks

Place your background music files here (MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, M4A).

Files are loaded alphabetically. Assign them to number keys (0-9) in the Music tab of ConductorSBN.

## Supported Formats
- MP3 (.mp3)
- WAV (.wav)
- OGG (.ogg)
- FLAC (.flac)
- M4A (.m4a) — requires ffmpeg on PATH

## Tips
- Use ambient/atmospheric music for best results
- Tracks loop automatically when you re-press the assigned key
- Use the volume slider in the Music tab to mix against sound effects
